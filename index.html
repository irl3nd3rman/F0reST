WHAT DAT BEAR DOIN

Me and my friends were once surprised by the presence of a Florinian Bear at the cemetery. Pretty scary experience for the fact that none of us has ever seen a real Bear other than small humble and cute Teddy Bears. I guess our connection with our Teddie's manifested their mother. Although we think that, what really manifested her was our cool and chill conversations and the smell of freshly rolled and burned weed. Maybe she was also chill like that. Since we were filled with fear to the point we couldn't feel our legs, we left silently in the hopes of surviving. Which we did. Sometimes I wonder what she was thinking of us. Were we a thread to her cubs? Was she cute and wanted to join the Girls Night Out? Many many theories. What I know for certain is her walk from the forest to us.

WHAT IF I WAS LITTLE

Since I was a child, I always wondered what it would be like to be a tinnie-tiny person, wandering around in the grass (which would probably be a forest for tinnie-tiny me) Ponds with water and flowers, grass and moss sounds like heaven for the little me. Having trips to walk through the big grass leaves and using mushrooms as shelter from the rain. Bugs and small animals to be my friends and companions to my travels. I still kinda wish I was little.
